About

Aging is the most risk factor for neurodegenerative disease, yet the knowledge about how it emerges across cells, tissues, and organ systems is still unknow.

Our work has contributed to this emerging paradigm by uncovering previously unrecognized modes of communication in the aging brain.

Building on this foundation, the Spatial Aging Biology & Disease Lab seeks to establish a new research direction.

To achieve this, we integrate proximity labeling–based cell- and tissue-specific proteomics.
