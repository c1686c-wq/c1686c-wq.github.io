Understanding aging mechanisms

Research

Mitochondrial Communication. We study how mitochondria communicate between neurons and glial cells, and how this interaction influences aging and stress adaptation.

Spatial Aging Biology. Our lab investigates how aging occurs differently across tissues, using spatial transcriptomics and imaging technologies.

Metabolic Regulation. We aim to understand how lipid metabolism and redox balance regulate cellular homeostasis during aging.
